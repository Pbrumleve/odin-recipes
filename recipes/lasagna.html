Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe from The Ultimate Student Cookbook: From Chicken to Chili by Tiffany Goodall.

It can be found on pages 96-97. There are three stages to this meal: the meat sauce, the cheese sauce, and the assembly.

Can be served with salad and garlic bread.

Serves: 6

Estimated total time is around 1 1/2 hours.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 4 tbsp (60 ml) olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed (4 small spoonfuls of minced garlic)
  • 1 onion, peeled and chopped finely
  • 1 red chili, seeded and chopped
  • 2 1/4 lb (1 kg) lean ground beef
  • a tbsp (15 ml) tomato paste or ketchup
  • 2 lb (900 g) canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) butter, for greasing
  • 1 box of ready-to-cook lasagna sheets
  • 5 oz (150 g) cheddar cheese grated for the topping

Cheese Sauce

  • 1/3 cup (75 g) butter
  • 1/2 cup (75 g) flour
  • 3 1/2 cups (825 ml) milk
  • 12 oz (350 g) cheddar cheese grated

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan and cook the garlic, onion and chili over medium heat. Cook for 5 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the beef and cook on high heat for 3 to 4 minutes until the beef is brown.
  4. Add the ketchup and tomatoes. Season and simmer over low heat for at least an hour.
  5. Meanwhile, make the cheese sauce. Put the butter and flour in a pan, and cook on medium heat, stirring for 1 to 2 minutes. (This is called a roux and will help to thicken the sauce.)
  6. Gradually whisk in the milk. Any lumps should dissolve as the milk becomes thicker. Stir for 4 to 5 minutes.
  7. Taste and season with salt and pepper, then add the cheese. Mix well until the cheese dissolves.
  8. Grease a large lasagna dish with butter.
  9. Then lay about 4 pasta sheets on the bottom of the dish. (Break a sheet up and use the pieces to fill in the gaps - this applies all the way through.)
  10. Spread 3 tbsp (45 ml) of meat sauce over the pasta and repeat with the cheese sauce.
  11. Assemble your next layer of pasta over the cheese sauce and repeat the process. You should be able to assemble 3-4 layers of pasta, depending on the size of your dish.
  12. Sprinkle the top with grated cheese.
  13. Cook in the oven for 35 to 45 minutes unitl golden brown.

Lasagna will keep assembled and uncooked in the refrigerator for 2 days. It will keep cooked and refrigerted for 3 days. To reheat, place in the oven at 300°F for 15 minutes.
