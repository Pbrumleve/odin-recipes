Cream Tacos

Description

This is a family friend recipe.

Serves: 6?

Estimated total time is around 1 1/2 hours.

Ingredients

  • 1 brick chili (hot)
  • 1 lb hamburger meat
  • 1 can rotel tomatoes
  • 2 16 oz cans chili hot beans
  • chili seasoning
  • 1 lb velveeta - chunks
  • small container of heavy whipping cream
  • tortilla chips
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • salsa
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Turn skillet on hot
  2. mix the following into skillet:
    • 1 brick chili (hot)
    • 1 lb hamburger meat
    • 1 can rotel tomatoes
    • 2 16 oz cans chili hot beans
    • chili seasoning
    • 1 lb velveeta - chunks
    • small container of heavy whipping cream
  3. Cook until browned, stirring occasionally
  4. Set up chips, lettuce, tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, and salsa in bowls
  5. Serve cream tacos on chips topped with whatever toppings are desired
